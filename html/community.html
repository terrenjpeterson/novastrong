A NOVA for the Future

Benefits to the Community

Expanding the NOVA facility will allow the club to reach and serve more members of the greater Richmond community.

An important part of NOVA’s long-range plan is to grow the sport of swimming. This goal is not only a cornerstone of NOVA’s mission, but it is also a primary tenet of USA Swimming. In order to do this, NOVA must have the facility resources to offer more to the community.

By expanding our facility, NOVA will be able to expand programs which directly impact the Richmond community. Additional facility resources will allow NOVA to:

  • Serve more children through our robust lessons program
  • Expand our outreach efforts to teach water safety and lessons to area children who would not otherwise be able to afford swim lessons
  • Introduce or grow programs to serve those with disabilities
  • Develop programs for the area’s older population, including the various centers within a few miles of the facility
  • Create a better environment for lessons, outreach and serving the elderly with a warm water facility
  • Investigate ways to partner with organizations offering rehabilitation services
  • Allow for the expansion of Henrico County Public Schools High School swimming partnership

    • Each of these opportunities will be evaluated as part of NOVA’s ongoing programming efforts. We will be working to establish the framework of these options as facility construction progresses in order to expand our programming as soon as possible after project completion.
