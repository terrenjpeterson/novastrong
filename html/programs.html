Our Mission

A NOVA for the future...

Competitive Youth Swim Team

Nearly 800 swimmers aged 6 to 18 compete as part of NOVA's competitive youth team. Our club has been ranked in the Top 10 of USA Swimming's Virtual Club Championship, which puts NOVA swimmers up against over 2,700 USA Swimming clubs in the nation, for the last 10 years. From the young swimmer participating in her first meet to our Olympic hopefuls, every swimmer is coached by our dedicated and decorated full-time coaching staff.

NOVA Masters Adult Competitive Team

NOVA's Masters team allows swimmers over the age of nineteen to keep swimming and competing. Our nationally-recognized Masters coach leads the group in structured training sessions. Competition is available for those who are interested, but many participants swim primarily for the workout and camaraderie.

Swimnova Lessons Program

NOVA's lessons program teaches almost 5,000 lessons each year to children aged three and up. Our lessons are based on our proprietary method which focuses on core body movements, leading to improvement over time as skills are added. We give lessons participants the skills needed not only to be safe in the water, but also to develop a lifelong love of swimming and the fitness it offers. Many swimmer eventually transition to our youth competitive team.

NOVAFit

NOVA also offers recreational lap swimming for adults through the NOVAFit program. Approximately 35-40 hours of open lap swimming are offered each week. Many older adults participate in this reasonably-priced program, allowing them to exercise according to their schedules.

Henrico County High School Club Swimming

NOVA is currently the sole partner of Henrico County Public Schools for club swimming. Almost 200 high school aged swimmers are participating in this program, started in 2013. HCPS eventually would like to offer swimming as a varsity sport, and NOVA is working to create a long-term partnership with HCPS for that effort.

Clinics

NOVA offers spring clinics to area summer league swim teams to prepare for the summer season. 425+ swimmers participate each year, gaining experience and valuable training advice from our coaching staff.
