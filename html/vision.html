A NOVA for the Future

THE PROJECT VISION

The expanded NOVA Aquatics Center will include the existing two pools and add an eight-lane, 50 meter by 25 yard, Olympic-sized, long-course pool. When facing the current building, the pool will be located to the left side of the existing structure, at the back of the parking lot. The current plan for the facility includes the pool, bleacher-style seating, new locker rooms, and a moveable bulkhead. Detailed drawings can be seen here.

The addition of the new building will necessitate additional parking as well. We are working with nearby landowners to secure additional parking space.

The cost of the project is currently estimated at $6.75 million; click here to learn more about project finances. The project cost will be covered by fundraising from individuals, businesses, and foundations, as well as bank loans and cash on hand. We began fundraising efforts in late 2014, and our fundraising total currently stands at over $600,000. To break ground, we need $2 million of the total cost of the project on hand.

In August 2014, NOVA issued a Request for Proposal from local architectural firms. The committee and board selected Moseley Architects in October 2014 to design the building. We are confident that we have made the best choice for our needs.

The conceptual renderings below show the current plan for the facility expansion. Please note, these are subject to change as the project progresses.
