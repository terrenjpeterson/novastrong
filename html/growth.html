A NOVA for the Future

WHY EXPAND?

The current NOVA Aquatics Center is unable to serve the needs of the club or the community. Our organization has grown tremendously over the last few years. We have grown from ninety competitive team swimmers to over 800. We currently teach almost 5,000 individual lessons each year. And, our other programming has grown, both in scope and in numbers. Revenues have increased significantly in the major areas of the business - team revenue, lessons revenue, and swim shop revenue. Our explosive growth over the last few years means the facility is limiting our ability to execute our mission.

Our desire to expand comes from two guiding principles: to provide a quality training environment to all of our NOVA swimmers, and to use our facility resources to meet the programming needs of the wider community. By expanding the NOVA Aquatics Center and gaining additional capacity, we will be able to further expand our offerings and our impact in the community, serving more swimmers of all ages and abilities and balancing the needs of the team with those of the community.
