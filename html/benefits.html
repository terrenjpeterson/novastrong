Our Mission

A NOVA for the furutre... The Capital Expansion Project.

Expanding the NOVA facility will benefit all of NOVA's current programming.

For the Competitive Youth Swim Team...

More space will have an immediate impact on our competitive youth team. NOVA is the only Top Twenty club in the country that does not have access to a 50M training facility. Having a long-course pool will allow our swimmers to train more effectively for the long-course season. NOVA will be able to host long-course meets, relieving burdensome and expensive travel for our families.

Perhaps more importantly, more lanes will mean less crowded practices for all team swimmers. We will be able to evaluate practice times and shift them to be more ideal for our families. We expect to be able to offer benefits to each of our training levels. Please note that the potential improvements listed below are subject to change.

Novice Groups

  • Offer an additional practice time at 4:30pm for all Novice groups; expand our 5:30pm practice time to offer an additional time for Novice Prep; offer four days of 5:30pm for Novice Silver and Novice Gold
    •
  • Eliminate shifting from one pool to the other during the middle of practices.
    •

    Age Groups

  • Alleviate lane overcrowding during some practices, particularly at 4:00pm in AGDS and AGDG, and at 5:30 with AGP/AGS.
  • Allow for lower swimmer to coach ratios.
  • Offer a later practice time for AGDS/AGDG to attract swimmers who currently do not have workable practice options.
  • Provide a consistent number of lanes for each group to ensure that lane shifting does not impact practice efficiencies.
  • Return AGP/AGS/AGG to earlier Friday practice times.
  • Increase retention in these groups.

    • Senior Groups

  • Allow for sufficient lane space for SDS to accomodate varied skill levels within group.
  • Institute more attractive practice time (return to 6:30pm and expand Friday option) for SDS.

    • All Team Members

  • Reduce practice changes or cancellations when NOVA is hosting home meets.
  • Allow for year-round long course training for elite swimmer and April-August long course training for all levels.
